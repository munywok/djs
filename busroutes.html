SCHOOL BUS ROUTES

SCHOOL TRANSPORT NETWORK

Bus routes are in operation to and from our both campuses. The service is provided at a reasonable additional cost and is a convenient option for all students living in and around Nairobi and Athi River locales.Buses are equipped with safety features and are monitored by trained staff to ensure a secure journey for every learner.
Learners receive safe, reliable and supervised transportation 5 days a week.We also provide transport for learners taking saturday extra-curricular activities. Please contact us for further details.

Please be aware that the School may adapt bus schedules to reflect demand. For information on the current and future bus routes, please contact the School and request an up-to-date transport outline.

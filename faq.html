DIAMOND JUNIOR FAQs 2025



Welcome to our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section! Here, you'll find answers to common queries about our school, including admissions, academic programs, extracurricular activities, and more. We aim to make your experience seamless and provide clarity on any concerns you might have. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to contact us directly—we’re here to help!

The School

+ When was Diamond Junior School founded
DJS was founded in 2009 with a commitment to equip & inspire each child to achieve their full God-given potential.
We celebrated a decade of Inspiring Excellence in January 2019.
+ What system do you use?
We use the Kenyan curriculum system – the Competency based curriculum. Please read more on the competencies here https://diamondjunior.com/about-us/

In Kindergarten, we have integrated both the Kenyan System and the Montessori curriculum to provide rich experiences for the children and best practices from both worlds.
+ What are the specific age requirements for the different classes?
Our Academic year runs from January to December. It is a requirement that children must have turned the requisite age for joining a class in January by 31st December in the previous year. Exceptions MAY be granted to those born between January – March.
  1. KG 1 - 2+years
  2. KG 2 – 3+ years
  3. PP 1 – 4+ years
  4. PP 2 – 5+ years
  5. GRADE 1 – 6+ years
  6. GRADE 2 – 7+ years
  7. GRADE 3 – 8+ years
  8. GRADE 4 – 9+ years
  9. GRADE 5 – 10+ years
  10. GRADE 6 – 11+ years
  11. GRADE 7 – 12+ years
  12. GRADE 8 - 13+ years
+ What is your highest grade?
This year (2024) our highest class is Grade 7 and we shall be moving with them to the subsequent classes progressively.
+ Do you offer Computer lessons and is it charged separately?
Yes, we do offer computer lessons for all our students as part of their classes and in addition to this, the 5+ year olds are also introduced to the basics of Robotics and Coding and Lego Education which advances as they move to the upper classes.
+ What extracurricular activities do you provide?
As a school, we are keen on according the children opportunities to discover and develop their talents & we have a wide array of extra-curricular activities such as – Swimming, Individual Music Instrument, Roller-skating, Ballet, Taekwondo, Football, Spelling Bee, Debate and Chess. These activities are optional and are charged separately from the tuition fees.
+ What is the youngest age of children that you admit?
Our youngest class are the Little Gems who join when they are 2 years old.
+ Are the children toilet trained?
Yes, the children in this age group are normally toilet trained and the progress is highly dependent on the support at home.
+ What is your mode of discipline?
As a values-based school, we endeavor to inculcate values in our children. One of our Key Pillars is – Character – and we aim to cultivate positive character in our children in order to raise responsible members of the society.
The children discuss with their class teachers in the classroom of their expected behavior through-out the term
We also resort to positive reward methods such as recognition in class and during assembly and awarding of points for positive behavior.

There are instances where we withdraw privileges, such as play time.
We do not allow corporal punishment instead we use other modes of correction such as boundary based discipline and behavior modification among others, that will enable us to get to the root cause of the issue, assist the child to properly express themselves and at the same time letting them know that what they have done or said was not right.
+ What are the School hours?
School starts at 8:00am and ends at 3:30pm. KG 1 & KG2 classes have the option of leaving at 12:30p.m (half day).
+ Do you provide meals for the children?
Yes, we provide balanced and nutritious meals for break time and lunch.
+ Are the children allowed to carry food from home?
Food from home is not permitted unless the child is on a special diet and/or on medical grounds.
+ What church is the school affiliated with?
Diamond Junior School is not affiliated to any specific Church, nevertheless it is a Christian school that upholds Christian values and is keen in instilling these values in each child.

The Transport Department

+ How far does your transport go?
Currently the farthest locations our transport services go include the following areas: Syokimau, Mbagathi way, Langata, Buruburu, Westlands, Athi River, Imara Daima and Embakasi.
+ What are your pick-up hours?
Our pick-up & drop off hours are dependent on how far one resides and the general traffic flow of the area.
+Is there an assistant on board during transport?
Yes, all our vehicles are accompanied by an onboard assistant while picking and dropping the children at all times.

All classes have a P.E lesson once a week whereby our coach takes them through age appropriate physical activities. Games are held every Friday. All children must be dressed in their full P.E/Games kit during these lessons.

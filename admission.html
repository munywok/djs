Admission Process



Register Your Interest With Us in a Few Simple Steps:

Step 1: Application

  • Click here to fill a Prospectus application form.
Step 2: Feedback From School.

  • The School will receive and review the application form.

  • The administrator will get intouch with you after reviewing the application form to schedule an entry assessment.
Step 3: Interview

  • Kindergarten learners are welcome to join a taster day and are assessed by the Head of the Kindergarten's section.

  • Grade 1 to Grade 8 learners are invited to attend an interview with the Head of Academics before meeting with the School Principal.
Step 4: Offer Letter

  • Confirmation of admission upon successful interview. The offer letter will be sent to the parents or legal guardians.
Step 5: Fees Payment

  • Parents/guardians pay are required to pay full school fees and caution fee upon receiving offer letter before admission.
Step 6: Admission

  • Your Child is fully admitted to Diamond Junior School.
